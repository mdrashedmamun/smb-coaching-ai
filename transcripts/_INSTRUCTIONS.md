# Transcript Upload Instructions

Please place your raw YouTube transcripts in this folder.

## Preferred Format
*   **File Extension**: `.txt` or `.md`
*   **Filename**: `video-1-topic.txt`, `video-2-topic.txt`
*   **Content**: Raw text is fine. Timestamps are optional but helpful for context.

## What happens next?
Once files are placed here, the AI will:
1.  Read the files.
2.  Extract the **Style Profile** (Voice, Tone, Metaphors).
3.  Extract the **Logic Rules** (If X -> Do Y).
4.  Generate a `candidate_rules.json` for your review.
