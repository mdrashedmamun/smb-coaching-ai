# Full Course Context & Material

## Module 1: Diagnose Existing Offers
### 1. Reality Check — The Amplification Trap
Imagine a singer with a shaky voice grabbing a megaphone. The louder they get, the worse it sounds. That’s exactly what happens when businesses amplify a weak offer with strong marketing...
### 2. Aisha and Daniel’s Status
Aisha (Skincare): Beautiful products, confused message. "Herbal skincare" vs "Clear skin".
Daniel (Marketing): "SEO Strategy" vs "22 Leads in 14 Days".
### 3. The Rebuild
- **Precision**: Aisha focused on "Women 20-35 looking to switch from chemicals".
- **Outcome**: "Clear, glowing skin in 30 days".
- **Clarity**: Daniel rewrote services to plain language results.

## Module 2: Diagnose Lead-to-Sales Cycle
### 1. Reality Check — The Leaky Bucket
You can have leads pouring in, but if the bucket has cracks, you lose them.
### 2. Aisha & Daniel
- Aisha: Treated every DM as a buyer. No filter.
- Daniel: Taking calls with anyone. Passive follow-up.
### 3. The Fix
- **Filters**: 3 DM questions (Budget, Status).
- **Tracking**: Daniel used a simple checklist.
- **Follow-up**: Day 1, 3, 7 rule.

## Module 3: Get More Leads (System)
### 1. Reality Check — The Empty Tank
The engine is tuned, but the tank is empty.
### 2. The Drought
Aisha needs 63 leads to hit targets but gets 20. Daniel needs 20 calls but gets 10.
### 3. The Fix
- **One Post**: Aisha "3 Skincare Mistakes" -> 47 DMs.
- **One Message**: Daniel sent 10 LinkedIn warm DMs -> 3 Calls.
- **System**: Identify sources, Map leads, Pick 1 fast path.

## Module 4: Get Better Leads (Quality)
### 1. Reality Check — Too Many Tables, No Orders
Restaurant is full of water-sippers.
### 2. The Noise
Aisha getting 50 DMs but only 12 buyers. Daniel booking 20 calls but half have no budget.
### 3. The Fix
- **Qualifiers**: "Starts at $X" in bio.
- **Pre-Filter**: Budget questions before booking.
- **Audit**: Cost of "Bad Leads" = Time Wasted.

## Module 5: Get Cheaper Leads (Cost)
### 1. Reality Check — The Whole Foods Shock
Great stuff, but the bill hurts.
### 2. The Cost Reality
Aisha paying $15/lead (profitable but inefficient). Daniel paying $45/lead on LinkedIn.
### 3. The Money Matters Playbook
- **Kill/Keep/Scale**: Audit every channel.
- **Referrals**: Daniel offered 10% off for referrals -> CPQL dropped 25%.
- **Content**: Aisha repurposes 1 blog -> 7 posts.

## Module 6: Get Leads Reliably (Consistency)
### 1. Reality Check — The Drought-Proof Business
Feast or Famine cycle.
### 2. The Volatility
Aisha: 300% variance. Daniel: 250% swing.
### 3. The Fix
- **Rhythm**: Weekly rituals, not daily hustle.
- **Safety Net**: What works if you get sick? (Referral SOP).
- **Pillars**: Content (Compounds), Outreach (Routine), Referrals (Easy).

## Module 7: Pitching
### 1. Reality Check — The Story That Shifts Shapes
Changing the story for every audience creates confusion.
### 2. The Breakdown
Aisha's grant app contradicted her website. Daniel's investor pitch lacked proof.
### 3. The Rebuild
- **Unified Narrative**: One core spine, adapted limbs.
- **10-Point Diagnostic**: Problem, Solution, Market, Traction, Team, Timing, etc.
- **Evidence**: Proof > Promise.
