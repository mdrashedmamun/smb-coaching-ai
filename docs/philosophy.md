# SMB Coaching AI — Core Philosophy

> **We help founders see the real constraint in their business and pull the highest-impact lever available, given who they are and where they operate.**

---

## The Hybrid Paradigm

We sequence two worldviews:

| Source | Optimizes For | How It Shows Up |
|--------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Paul Graham** | Discovery, learning speed, founder-first | UI buckets as identity anchors |
| **Warren Buffett** | Durability, cash flow quality, economic truth | 5-axis scoring engine |

Founders:
1. Enter through **identity** (Paul)
2. See truth through **economics** (Buffett)
3. Act through **leverage**, not ideology

---

## What We Do

- **Clarity over confusion**
- **Leverage over hustle**
- **Diagnosis over advice**
- **Decision-making over motivation**

---

## What We Do NOT Do

- We do not evangelize SaaS, recurring revenue, or scale
- We do not prescribe business models — **we reveal leverage when evidence supports it**
- We do not tell SMB founders to become tech companies
- We do not collapse operator realities into investor abstractions
- We do not confuse distribution channels with business models

---

## The 5-Axis Engine (Non-Negotiable)

Every business is scored on:

1. **Labor vs Product Leverage** — Is revenue capped by headcount?
2. **Fixed vs Variable Cost** — How does margin behave at scale?
3. **One-Time vs Recurring Revenue** — Is there predictable cash flow?
4. **Distribution Dependency** — Who owns the customer relationship?
5. **Moat Type** — Brand, switching cost, regulation, data, or cost advantage?

---

## The 90-Day Lever (Required Output)

Every diagnostic MUST include:

1. **Primary Constraint** — Derived from axes
2. **Why This Constraint Exists** — 1–2 sentences, plain language
3. **90-Day Lever** — Single highest-impact action
4. **What Not to Fix Yet** — Prevents wasted motion

### Rules
- **One lever only.** If you give three, you've given zero.
- **Must map to axis constraint.** No vibes. No "best practices."

---

## The Line

> Evangelism starts with an answer and looks for justification.
> Diagnosis starts with constraints and reveals options.

We are the second.
