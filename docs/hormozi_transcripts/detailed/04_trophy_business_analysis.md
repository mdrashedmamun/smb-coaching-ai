# 04 Trophy and Plaques Business (51 min) - Detailed Analysis

## Snapshot
- Business: Custom trophies/plaques; ecommerce + retail
- Revenue: ~1.72M; Profit: ~110k; Net margin: ~6.4%
- Core issues: thin margin, channel overload, unclear model choice

## Alex's Diagnostic Flow (as observed)
1) Margin reality check signals pricing and focus problems.
2) Channel sprawl review: retail + ecommerce + viral content.
3) Levers defined: consolidation, pricing, media.
4) Force decision on business model before scaling.

## Prescriptions (ordered)
- Consolidate: close losing retail location, reduce operational drag.
- Pick a business model (viral consumer vs recurring B2B) and commit.
- Switch to value-based pricing and continuous price tests.
- Build retention via email/SMS to drive repeat orders.
