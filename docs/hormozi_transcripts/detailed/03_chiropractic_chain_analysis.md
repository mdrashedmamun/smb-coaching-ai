# 03 Chiropractic Chain (42 min) - Detailed Analysis

## Snapshot
- Business: Multi-location chiropractic clinics
- Revenue: ~5.2M; Profit: ~1.2M; Net margin: ~23%
- Leads: ~35/mo per location; Show rate: ~80%; Close rate: ~71%
- CAC: ~700; LTV: ~3,400 (about 4.8:1)
- Core issues: lead flow consistency, sales infrastructure, people ops

## Alex's Diagnostic Flow (as observed)
1) Revenue/margin check confirms business health.
2) Offer review shows premium packages already in place.
3) Funnel metrics show strong close rates -> possible price power.
4) Constraint ranking: lead flow, sales infrastructure, people ops.
5) Plan prioritized by impact and ease (ICE order).

## Prescriptions (ordered)

- Add low-ticket lead magnet and tighten ad copy.
- Standardize sales process and verification steps.
- Replicate best-performing location playbook.
- Invest in local SEO for durable lead flow.
