# 07 Eyelash Salon Chain (57 min) - Detailed Analysis

## Snapshot
- Business: Multi-location eyelash salons
- Revenue: ~2.3M; Profit: ~644k; Net margin: ~28%
- Problems: lead flow, service quality, partner misalignment

## Alex's Diagnostic Flow (as observed)
1) Stake and bandwidth check: partner tension is execution blocker.
2) Offer and retention check: rebooking + membership.
3) Margin mechanics: high labor share (50% topline to artists).
4) Prioritize training and upsell mechanics.

## Prescriptions (ordered)
- Resolve partnership misalignment first.
- Implement BAFFM rebooking (book next visit before leaving).
- Improve membership offer and upsell process.
- Train team and install cadence to improve step-2 take rate.
- Add attribution + ads only after model is stable.
